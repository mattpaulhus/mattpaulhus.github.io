Providing Training for 750 Service Members

Leading, planning, and influencing an important Army Reserve Exercise

September 2019 Matt Paulhus

Mission

Officer in Charge of Nationwide Move 2019. Provide management of operational-level movement control and transportation operations in the United States to create training opportunities for identified Nationwide Move units and reduce the transportation costs for equipment hauls.

How

I led a team of three full-time individuals responsible for the Nationwide Move 2019 program. I primarily provided strategic vision, planning, analysis, reports, and recommendations related to program status, execution, and performance.

Result

  • Saved the Army $1.9 million in equipment transportation costs with a net O&M savings of $536k
  • Directly trained over 750 USAR and NG Soldiers and shipped unit equipment to and from 7 major Army exercises
  • The 29 real-orld missions supported 7 U.S. exercises and 1 Canada-U.S. exercise
  • Provided annual training for 16 Army Reserve and National Guard units

What I learned

How to provide strategic vision, planning, and analysis to all levels. How to think broadly and work with a large number of departments and multiple echelons, ranging from individual soldiers to one-star generals.
